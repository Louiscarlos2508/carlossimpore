# Persona: Tech-Lead Tanya (The Partnership Scout)

## Context & Personality
Tanya is a 35-year-old CTO or Senior Technical Director. She is a deep-stack expert who values **resilience**, **clean architecture**, and **system-level thinking**. She is often the one who makes the final decision on high-stakes technical partners. She is wary of the "AI hype" and looks for substance over surface.

## A Day in Tanya's Life
- **Morning**: Auditing a feature that an "AI Developer" built, only to find it has no error handling and breaks the real-time sync.
- **Afternoon**: Architecting a mission-critical multi-tenant system and looking for a specialist who can handle the "Engine" (backend + sync) + "Hybrid" (AI orchestration).
- **Night**: Reading technical articles and scouring portfolios for actual **Proof of Concept** videos and architecture diagrams.

## Goals & Aspirations
- **Zero Debt Architecture**: To build systems that are "Titan-grade" from day one.
- **Strategic Partnership**: To find a reliable, innovative "Hybrid-Engine" specialist who can be trusted with the most complex modules.
- **Resilient AI**: To move from "Prompt Engineering" to robust, agentic workflows that have built-in telemetry and failure recovery.

## Driving Forces (Push & Pull)
- **PUSH (Away)**: Surface-level AI "script kiddies." High technical debt. Scalability bottlenecks in existing apps. Fragile tech that fails without internet.
- **PULL (Toward)**: **Titan-grade Engineering**. Offline-first sync. Expert-level architectural depth. Proven technical reliability.

## Key Interaction: The "Autonomy Lab"
Tanya needs to see the **Bedrock**. She cares about the code, the sync, and the telemetry. She wants to see your **"Project Heartbeat"** and your architecture diagrams because *that* is what she’s buying.
