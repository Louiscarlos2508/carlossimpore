# UX Scenario 01: From Burnout to Autonomy

## Scenario Metadata
- **Persona**: **Solopreneur Sam** (The Scale-Hunter)
- **Primary Goal**: To discover how a "Hybrid-Engine" can automate his manual client-sync burnout.
- **Conversion Point**: AI Briefing Architect (Intake).

## Strategic Trigger Application
- **PUSH (Friction)**: "Manual Sync Burnout" and Scaling Anxiety.
- **PULL (Flow)**: **The "10s Flow"** (Efficiency-to-the-Max).

## Linear Sunshine Path

| # | Page / View | User Action | System Reaction (Extraordinary Value) |
|---|-------------|-------------|---------------------------------------|
| 1 | **Home / Hero** | Sam lands on the site from a link about "Scaling without Hiring." | **Hero Hook**: Displays a dynamic ticker of "Manual Hours Reclaimed" and the "Hybrid-Engine" positioning. |
| 2 | **Home / Scroller** | Scrolls to find how this works for his specific situation. | **Context Injector**: Section specifically for Solopreneurs showing "Before" (Brittle) vs. "After" (Engine). |
| 3 | **Case Study: The 10s Treasurer** | Clicks the case study for concrete evidence. | **ROI Reveal**: Shows the "10-Minute to 10-Second" transformation with a live benchmark video. |
| 4 | **The Decision Log UI** | Clicks "See the Logic" within the case study. | **Interactive Transparency**: Sam can toggle "Agent Logic" to see how the AI made a decision, building trust in the backend. |
| 5 | **AI Briefing Architect** | Clicks "Architect My Engine" call-to-action. | **Intelligent Intake**: Instead of a form, an AI interview begins to assess his specific scaling bottlenecks. |

## Success Metric
Sam confirms "Value Clarity" by starting the AI Briefing flow within 60 seconds of landing.
