# UX Scenario 10: The Engine Hub Tour

## Scenario Metadata
- **Persona**: **General Innovation Scout / Explorer**
- **Primary Goal**: To understand what a "Hybrid-Engine" actually is and how it applies at scale.
- **Trigger**: Innovation Authority & Curiosity.

## Linear Sunshine Path

| # | Page / View | User Action | System Reaction (Extraordinary Value) |
|---|-------------|-------------|---------------------------------------|
| 1 | **Home / Grid** | Visitor lands on the "Hybrid-Engine Hub." | **Hub Presentation**: Visual grid of "Titan" (Resilience) vs. "Accelerator" (AI). |
| 2 | **Service Catalog** | Clicks "Solutions" to see the engine's reach. | **Multi-Domain Evidence**: Cards showing ERP, Treasury, and AI Agency applications. |
| 3 | **Decision Log** | Clicks "AI Logic Demo." | **Interactive proof** of how the engine processes business rules. |
| 4 | **The Autonomy Lab** | Navigates to the live monitor section. | **Proof of Existence**: Sees live heartbeats of the engine's sub-systems. |
| 5 | **AI Briefing Architect** | Starts a general inquiry. | **Authority Capture**: Inquiry to explore collaboration or custom builds. |

## Success Metric
Visitor spends 3+ sessions and shares the "Engine" concept with their circle.
